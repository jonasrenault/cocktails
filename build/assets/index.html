212

60 ml Partida Reposado Tequila

30 ml Aperol

60 ml Ruby Red Grapefruit Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled Collins glass filled with ice. Garnish with an orange twist.

Alexander

30 ml Gin or Brandy

30 ml Dark Crème de Cacao

60 ml Heavy Cream

Shake and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with nutmeg.

Algonquin

60 ml Rye Whiskey

25 ml Dry Vermouth

25 ml Pineapple Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. No garnish.

Americano

45 ml Carpano Antica Sweet Vermouth

45 ml Campari

Build in a chilled Collins glass. Add ice and top with club soda. Garnish with half an orange wheel.

Apple Crumble

45 ml Havana Club 7-Year Old Rum

30 ml Apple Cider

25 ml Lemon Juice

15 ml Simple Syrup

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled Collins glass filled with ice. Garnish with an apple slice sprinkled with grated cinnamon and nutmeg.

Aviation

60 ml Beefeater Gin

25 ml Lemon Juice

15 ml Luxardo Maraschino Liqueur

10 ml Crème de Violette

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a flamed lemon peel.

Bee's Knee

60 ml Gin

25 ml Lemon Juice

25 ml Honey Syrup

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. No garnish.

Black/White Russian

45 ml Vodka

15 ml Kahlúa

Pour into a chilled rocks glass with ice. Add 30 ml cream for a White Russian.

Blackthorn

60 ml Irish Whiskey

30 ml Dry Vermouth

2 dashes Angostura Bitters

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled, Absinthe-rinsed martini glass. Garnish with a flamed lemon peel.

Blood and Sand

45 ml Famous Grouse Blended Scotch Whisky

15 ml Cherry Heering

15 ml Sweet Vermouth

25 ml Orange Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a flamed orange peel.

Bloody Mary

45 ml Vodka

15 ml Lemon Juice

2 dash Worcestershire sauce

4 dash Tabasco sauce

120 ml Tomato Juice

Salt & Pepper

Roll in a mixing glass. Pour in a Collins glass with ice. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Bobby Burns

60 ml Scotch

25 ml Martini Sweet Vermouth

10 ml Bénédictine

2 dashes Peychaud's Bitters

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Serve with a shortbread cookie.

Bramble

45 ml Gin

25 ml Lemon Juice

25 ml Simple Syrup

25 ml Crème de mure

Shake the gin, lemon and syrup with ice. Strain into a chilled Collins glass with ice. Top with crème de mure. Garnish with raspberries.

Caipirinha

60 ml Cachaça

Half Lime, quartered

2 bar-spoons brown sugar

Muddle the lime and sugar. Shake with ice and dump into a chilled rocks glass. No garnish.

Charlie Chaplin

30 ml Sloe Gin

30 ml Apricot Brandy

30 ml Lime Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lime peel.

Clover Leaf

60 ml Plymouth Gin

20 ml Lemon Juice

15 ml Simple Syrup

10 ml Raspberry Syrup

1 Egg White

Dry shake, then shake with ice and strain into a chilled egg coupe. Garnish with a mint sprig

Conquistador

30 ml Matusalem Gran Reserva Rum

30 ml Siembra Azul Blanco Tequila

25 ml Simple Syrup

15 ml Lemon Juice

15 ml Lime Juice

2 dashes House Orange Bitters

1 Egg White

Dry shake, then shake with ice and strain into a chilled egg coupe. No garnish.

Corpse Reviver

25 ml Plymouth Gin

25 ml Cointreau

25 ml Lillet Blanc

25 ml Lemon Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled absinthe rinsed coupe. No garnish.

Cosmopolitan

45 ml Citron Vodka

15 ml Cointreau

10 ml Lime Juice

30 ml Cranberry Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a flamed orange peel.

Daiquiri

45 ml Light Rum

25 ml Lime Juice

20 ml Simple Syrup

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with a lime wheel.

Duboudreau Cocktail

60 ml Rye Whiskey

25 ml Dubonnet Rouge

10 ml Fernet-Branca

10 ml St.Germain Elderflower Liqueur

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with a lemon twist.

Espresso Martini

45 ml Vodka

20 ml Kahlúa

10 ml White Crème de Cacao

30 ml Espresso

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with coffee beans.

Espresso Martini (Alternate)

60 ml Bailey's Irish Cream

30 ml Vodka

30 ml Espresso

15 ml Amaretto

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with chocolate syrup.

French 75

30 ml Tanqueray Gin (or Cognac)

15 ml Lemon Juice

15 ml Simple Syrup

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled Champagne flute. Top with Champagne.

French Martini

60 ml Vodka

15 ml Chambord

45 ml Pineapple Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. No garnish.

Gimlet

60 ml Plymouth Gin

30 ml Lime Cordial

Shake and strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with a lime wheel.

Gin rickey

60 ml Gin

15 ml Lime Juice

Build in a Collins glass filled with ice. Top with club soda. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Gold Rush

60 ml Elijah Craig 12-Year-Old Bourbon

30 ml Honey Syrup

25 ml Lemon Juice

Shake with ice and strain over a large cube into a chilled rocks glass. No garnish.

Hanky Panky

60 ml Beefeater Gin

45 ml Sweet Vermouth

10 ml Fernet Branca

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with an orange twist.

Hemingway Daiquiri

45 ml Light Rum

25 ml Lime Juice

15 ml Luxardo Maraschino Liqueur

15 ml Grapefruit Juice

Shake and strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with a lime wheel.

Irish Coffee

45 ml Irish Whiskey

1 shot of Coffee

2 bar-spoons Brown Sugar

Combine in a glass. Top with Hot Water and ladle 1 inch of hand whipped cream on top. Garnish with coffee beans.

Long Island Iced Tea

15 ml Gin

15 ml Vodka

15 ml Light Rum

15 ml Tequila

15 ml Triple Sec

20 ml Lemon Juice

15 ml Simple Syrup

Shake with ice. Strain in a Collins glass and top with coke. Garnish with a lemon wedge.

Mai Tai

60 ml Aged Rum

30 ml Lime Juice

15 ml Orange Curaçao

15 ml Orgeat

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled rocks glass filled with ice. Garnish with a mint sprig.

Manhattan

60 ml Wild Turkey Rye Whiskey

30 ml Martini Sweet Vermouth

2 dash Angostura Bitters

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a cherry.

Margarita

60 ml Tequila

25 ml Lime Juice

20 ml Cointreau

10 ml Agave Syrup

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled rocks glass filled with ice or a chilled martini glass with salted rim.

Martinez

45 ml Gin

45 ml Dolin Sweet Vermouth

10 ml Luxardo Maraschino Liqueur

2 dashes of Adam Elmegirab's Boker's Bitters (Substitute Angosture Bitters)

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with an orange twist.

Mint Julep

60 ml Bourbon

15 ml Simple Syrup

8 Mint Leaves

Muddle the leaves and simple syrup in a chilled julep cup. Add crushed ice and bourbon. Garnish with 3 mint sprigs.

Mojito

60 ml Light Rum

30 ml Simple Syrup

25 ml Lime Juice

8 Mint Leaves

Muddle the mint leaves and simple syrup in a Collins glass. Add the rum, lime juice and crushed ice. Top with soda water. Garnish with a mint sprig.

Moscow Mule

45 ml Smirnoff Black Vodka

2 Lime Wedges, squeezed

4 Mint Leaves, slaped

Build in a collins glass with ice and top with ginger beer. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Negroni

30 ml Beefeater Gin

30 ml Campari

30 Martini Sweet Vermouth

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled coupe or over ice in a chilled rocks glass. Garnish with a flamed orange peel.

Old Fashioned

60 ml Wild Turkey Rye Whiskey

1 White Sugar Cube

2 dashes Angostura Bitters

Dissolve the sugar cube with the bitters and soda water. Add the whiskey and stir with ice. Strain over one large cube into a chilled rocks glass. Garnish with an orange peel.

Old Maid

60 ml Plymouth Gin

30 ml Lime Juice

25 ml Simple Syrup

6 Mint Leaves

4 Slices Cucumber

Muddle the mint and cucumber with the syrup. Add everything else and shake with ice then strain over one large cube into a chilled rocks glass. Garnish with a mint sprig poked through a slice of cucumber.

Pimm's Cup

60 ml Pimm's #1 Cup

20 ml Lemon Juice

Build in a Collins glass filled with ice. Top with ginger ale. Garnish with a cucumber spear.

Rattlesnake

60 ml Rye Whiskey

30 ml Lemon Juice

25 ml Simple Syrup

1 Egg White

Dry shake, then shake with ice and strain into a chilled Absinthe rinsed coupe. No garnish.

Rob Roy

75 ml Famous Grouse Blended Scotch Whiskey

30 ml Dolin Sweet Vermouth

2 dashes Angostura Bitters

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a lemon twist.

Rosebud

30 ml Vanilla Vodka

15 ml Lemon Juice

15 ml Passion Fruit Juice

30 ml Cranberry Juice

30 ml Pineapple Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with rose petals.

Rosita

45 ml Reposado Tequila

15 ml Sweet Vermouth

15 ml Dry Vermouth

15 ml Campari

1 dash Angostura Bitters

Stir with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with an orange twist.

Rusty Nail

60 ml Famous Grouse Blended Scotch Whiskey

20 ml Drambuie

Pour the scotch into a chilled rocks glass with ice. Float the Drambuie on top. No garnish.

Sazerac

30 ml V.S. Cognac

30 ml Rye Whiskey

15 ml Simple Syrup

3 dashes Peychaud's Bitters

2 dashes Angostura Bitters

Stir with ice. Strain into a chilled, Absinthe-rinsed rocks glass. Garnish with a lemon peel.

Sidecar

60 ml Cognac

20 ml Cointreau

20 ml Lemon Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled, half sugarrimmed coupe or a chilled rocks glass with ice.

Singapore Sling

45 ml Plymouth Gin

15 ml Cherry Heering

10 ml Cointreau

10 ml Bénédictine

10 ml Lime Juice

60 ml Pineapple Juice

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled Collins glass filled with ice. Top with soda. Garnish with a lemon slice and a cherry.

Southside

60 ml Gin

25 ml Lime Juice

25 ml Simple Syrup

4 Mint Leaves

Muddle the mint and syrup. Add everything else then shake with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. No garnish

Swizzle

45 ml Rum

10 ml Falernum

15 ml Lime Juice

15 ml Simple Syrup

Dash of Angostura Bitters

Build in a tall glass with crushed ice. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Tom Collins

60 ml Hayman's Old Tom Gin

25 ml Lemon Juice

15 ml Simple Syrup

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled Collins glass filled with ice. Top with club soda and garnish with a cherry and orange slice.

Vesper Martini

45 ml Plymouth Gin

15 ml Belvedere Vodka

20 ml Lillet Blanc

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with an orange peel.

Vieux Carré

30 ml Rye Whiskey

30 ml Cognac

30 ml Sweet Vermouth

10 ml Bénédictine

1 dash Angostura Bitters

1 dash Peychaud's Bitters

Stir with ice and strain over one large cube into a chilled glass. No garnish.

Vieux Mot

45 ml Plymouth Gin

20 ml Lemon Juice

15 ml St. Germain Elderflower Liqueur

15 ml Simple Syrup

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. No garnish.

White Lady

60 ml Beefeater Gin

25 ml Cointreau

25 ml Lemon Juice

10 ml Simple Syrup

1 Egg White

Dry shake, then shake with ice and strain into a chilled coupe. No garnish.

Zombie

30 ml Light Rum

30 ml Dark Rum

30 ml Overproof Rum

15 ml Lime Juice

15 ml Lemon Juice

45 ml Orange Juice

45 ml Passion Fruit Juice

10 ml Grenadine

2 dashes Angostura bitters

Shake with ice and strain into a chilled tiki mug. Garnish with a mint sprig.
