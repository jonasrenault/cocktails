Vionta Albarino

Crisp and refreshing with a long creamy lemony finish. Hints of melon, grapefruit and apricot on the nose. Albarino wines are very aromatic, sweet smelling of peaches and apricots. Similar to Viognier but with much less weight.

Babich Sauvignon Blanc

Delicate floral notes intermingle with ripe melon and apple on the nose. The flavours are clean and precise with a cleansing natural acidity finish.

Stumpy Gully Chardonnay

Intense pear and lifter orange blossom on the nose. It has subtle oak flavours, ripe peach and buttered toast richness. It is well rounded with a clean fresh finish.

Jean-Luc Mader Pinot Blanc

It is pale in color and showing ripe stone fruits, pear and a touch of spice. It is a soft, gentle easy drinking wine with a crisp and refreshing finish. Pinot blanc has virtually no darkness in its skin and is used to make refreshing dry white wines.

Delatite Riesling

Classic Riesling style with rich spicy aromas of cinnamon and floral notes from fermenting with wild yeast. Dry with hints of fresh apple and citrus flavours.

Tomich Hill Rose

Light bodied with abundant wild strawberry and raspberry fruit flavours with an elegant, crisp finish.

Yeringberg Viognier

Aromas of honey, peach and apricot. Palate is creamy with ripe textural flavours of peach.
